Hi Dana,

Thanks for the note, and welcome aboard. I can see exactly what happened: the roadmap link you sent Jules is still on the default read-only view, so re-ranking and comments are grayed out on their end even though everything works fine on yours.

Here's the fix:

1. Open the same link you already sent Jules and click **Manage access** in the top right.
2. Enter their email and set the role to **Collaborator** rather than Viewer.
3. Jules will get an email invite. Once they accept it, that exact link becomes interactive on their end - dragging items to re-rank and leaving comments both included.

Nothing else changes on your side. Your own view stays exactly as it was, and the link stays read-only for anyone else you have shared it with unless you add them as a collaborator too.

This should be in place well before Monday's planning meeting - invites typically land within a minute or two of adding someone.

Let me know if the invite does not come through, or if anything else comes up before then.
