We are glad you are here. Tidemark launches next week, and we wanted to introduce it properly - not with a list of features, but with a conversation.

You are probably building something with a small team. You talk to customers, collect what they tell you, and then you face the hard part: turning all of that scattered input into a plan your team and your stakeholders can actually trust. The notes are in the chat tool, the tickets are in the tracker, the sticky note from last quarter is somewhere you have stopped looking. You know what your customers want - mostly - but getting it organized and in front of the right people takes longer than the insight deserves.

Tidemark gathers your feedback from wherever it lives and helps you surface what matters most. It produces a ranked, shareable roadmap you can put in front of anyone - your team, a board member, a reporter. It does not replace your judgment; that is yours. It just gives your judgment something clear to work with.

This is not a tool built for large organizations with full-time researchers. It was made for the teams where one person is doing three jobs and customer feedback is valuable but messy to manage.

If you want early access, the link below will take you straight there. We will walk you through setup personally, and we genuinely want to know what you think.

We are glad you found us.
