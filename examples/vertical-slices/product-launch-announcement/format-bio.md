Marisol Veen is Head of Product at Tidemark, where she has led the product from private beta through its June 30 public launch. Before Tidemark, she was a senior product manager at Bramwell Analytics, where she once consolidated four separate feedback channels into a single monthly prioritization review by hand - the same problem Tidemark now solves for other teams.

At Tidemark, Marisol set the launch to lead with that problem, scattered feedback with no shared ranked view, rather than a product-category label. She ran the twenty-two team early-access cohort through the full feedback-to-roadmap workflow ahead of general availability - every team completed it without filing a support ticket - and defined the three-tier pricing model that dropped the sales-call requirement from the free plan.

Marisol holds a B.S. in Business Administration from Brightwater College and is based in Portland, Oregon. She is available for press inquiries at marisol.veen@tidemark.io.
