"Our roadmap meeting used to be the worst ninety minutes on our calendar. Feedback for Harrow Digital was scattered everywhere: chat threads, a spreadsheet nobody owned, an email folder, a ticket tracker. Half of every meeting went to arguing about whose version was right before we could even start deciding anything. I ran Tidemark's setup wizard one afternoon, and by our next meeting the whole team was looking at the same ranked list instead of six different ones. That meeting ran twenty-two minutes. We still disagreed sometimes, but for once we were disagreeing about the same facts instead of different ones. If your team spends more time re-establishing what customers said than deciding what to build next, get this. Setup took me under an hour."

- Lena Voss, Co-founder, Harrow Digital
