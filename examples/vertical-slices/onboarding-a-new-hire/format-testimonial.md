*Northlane Systems careers page, Engineering section - new-hire quote*

"At my last two jobs, onboarding meant a laptop, a login, and a wiki page nobody had updated in a year. I assumed Northlane would be the same. It wasn't. Arjun was my buddy from day one, and it was actually blocked on his calendar. He ran a checklist and didn't check anything off until I'd verified it myself, so access problems never sat in a queue. My first real change was scoped before I even started, one service, low risk, so I wasn't guessing what was safe to touch. It shipped and deployed by the end of week two, and by then I already knew who to call, not just where to look. Two weeks isn't long enough to judge a whole company, but it's long enough to tell whether people mean what they say. Here, they did. I would take this offer again."
- Priya Rao, Software Engineer, Backend Services Team at Northlane Systems
