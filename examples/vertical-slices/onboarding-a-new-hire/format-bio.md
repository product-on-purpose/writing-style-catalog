# Priya Rao
*Team directory profile - Backend Services, Northlane Systems*

Priya Rao is a Software Engineer on the Backend Services team at Northlane Systems, joining in June 2026 after nearly four years at Larkspur Systems. At Larkspur, she owned the backend systems behind billing and invoicing, redesigning alert routing across two monitoring tools to cut duplicate pages and migrating a legacy nightly batch job to an event-driven pipeline that now runs in minutes instead of hours. She also mentored two newly hired engineers through their first on-call rotations, pairing on incident response until each could run primary solo.

At Northlane, she has moved quickly: she traced her first production request end to end within her opening week and shipped her first change to the service before the end of her second. Priya holds a B.S. in Computer Science from Ridgeline State University and works remotely from the US.
