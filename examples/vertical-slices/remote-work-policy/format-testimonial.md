*Collected for the hybrid-anchor landing page, "What adopters say" section.*

"We'd been circling the same argument for over a year: office-first leadership wanted five days, our remote hires (half the team, three states away) said they'd leave if we mandated it. I pulled the hybrid-anchor framework off Millbrook's landing page and used the quick-start exactly as published, Tuesday and Thursday, nothing else changed for the first month. The thing I didn't expect: our new hires stopped asking the same onboarding questions three times in three different Slack channels, because the people who could answer them were finally in the room on the same two days. We kept every remote hire we were afraid of losing. I'd start with the two days exactly as written before you touch anything else."
- Renata Cole, VP of People, a mid-size healthcare-scheduling company
