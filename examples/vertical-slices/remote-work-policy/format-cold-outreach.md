**Subject:** The third design, not a compromise

You wrote a few months back that you had stopped covering return-to-office policy, because every version came down to the same two options wearing different logos: mandate everyone back, or let the whole thing go fully remote. You said you would pick the subject up again the day somebody showed you a third design worth taking seriously.

I lead the working group that just finished arguing our way to exactly that: two mandatory anchor days a week, Tuesday and Thursday, flexible on everything else, and we published it as an open framework called hybrid-anchor rather than filing it away as an internal memo. It is not a compromise between your two camps but a third position with its own logic.

Worth a look before I send it any further, or is the subject still closed?

Priya Ahluwalia
Wrote the hybrid-anchor framework
