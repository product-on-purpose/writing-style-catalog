Being told the arrangement was flexible at hire, and then facing two mandatory office days now, is a fair thing to be upset about - this deserves a direct answer, not a form reply.

We won't pretend two anchor days a week is a small ask for someone who relocated or rearranged childcare around full flexibility. That cost is real, and we said so when we adopted the policy. Anchor days are Tuesday and Thursday; outside of roles that were remote-eligible at hire, they are not optional.

Your specific situation is something we can work through, but not here. There is a documented exception process through your manager and the people team - reach out, and we will look at it honestly.
