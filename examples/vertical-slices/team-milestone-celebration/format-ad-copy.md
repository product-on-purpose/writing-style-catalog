# Ad Copy - Halyard Engineering Brand Campaign

**Campaign:** Engineering brand / recruiting, built around the checkout rebuild's close-out
**Audience:** Senior backend and infrastructure engineers evaluating where to work next
**Internal name:** Project Halyard (the ads use plain language; codenames stay internal)

Five variations testing which proof point lands hardest before spend commits to one angle. Priya signs off on the specific-numbers unit before anything goes external.

**Headline:** We spent fourteen months not breaking checkout.

**Body:** Parallel systems, gradual traffic, two near-misses caught before a single customer saw them. That is how this team ships.
