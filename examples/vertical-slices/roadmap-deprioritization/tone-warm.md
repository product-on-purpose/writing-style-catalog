I want to be honest with you, and I want to do it personally - not through a release note or a changelog entry.

You were promised Insights by the end of Q3. We are not going to deliver it on that timeline, and you deserve a straight explanation of why.

Earlier this year, a mandatory migration of our billing infrastructure surfaced work we had underestimated. That migration is not optional - it underpins how we charge every customer and process every invoice, and it had to be completed correctly. By the time we understood its full scope, it had consumed the engineering capacity we had reserved for Insights. We faced a choice: ship Insights on schedule in a state we knew was incomplete, or delay it and ship something you could actually use. We chose the delay.

Insights will be in your hands in Q1. We are not putting a softer date on it; Q1 is the target and we are treating it as a commitment.

Here is what we are doing before September closes: you will have a CSV export of the underlying Insights data, available directly from your account. It is not the in-app experience we built toward, and we know that. But it means you do not have to wait until Q1 to start working with the data your team cares about. You can pull it into your own tools now, on your own schedule.

I am glad you are still with us through this. Your patience is not something I take for granted, and I want Insights to earn it when it arrives.
