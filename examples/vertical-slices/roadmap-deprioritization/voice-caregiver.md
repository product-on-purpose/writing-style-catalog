Insights is not shipping in Q3. We know you have already committed to this with your customers and with your teams. That is the part that makes this message hard to write, and harder to receive.

Here is what happened. The billing migration we ran this quarter grew larger than it was scoped, and it consumed the engineering capacity we had reserved for Insights. We had a choice: ship Insights on the original date with key pieces missing, or move it to a place where we can build it properly. Shipping something half-built to customers who were specifically promised it was not something we could do.

Insights moves to Q1. That is the honest target and we are not hedging it. Q1 is where the work lands.

What we can give you this quarter is a CSV export of the underlying data - the same data Insights will surface in the dashboard. It is not the experience we planned, and we know it is not what you sold. But it is real data. It is ready before the quarter ends. Your customers can open it in whatever analysis tool they already use.

You do not have to respond to this today. If you need a call to help you talk to your customers about the change, we will get on the calendar. If a draft message would help, we will write one. We are here for the hard part of this.
