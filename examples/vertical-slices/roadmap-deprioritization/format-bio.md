# Maya Chen

*Meridian > About > Leadership*

Maya Chen is Product Lead at Meridian, where she owns product strategy and stakeholder communication for the platform's usage-analytics surface. When a mandatory billing-system migration collided with the Q3 commitment for the Insights dashboard, Maya chose to defer the feature rather than ship it without the saved-view and scheduled-report capabilities the committed customers had specifically asked for, and had a CSV export of the same underlying data live for those customers within two weeks. She briefed sales and customer success ahead of the written deferral notice, secured leadership sign-off on the revised target before it reached a single account, and locked the full dashboard scope for next quarter with engineering. The instinct behind that call is not new: at Northlake Software, Maya introduced a quarterly capacity-review checkpoint between engineering and sales leadership to catch exactly this kind of conflict before it reaches a customer, and she has run some version of that check at every team she has led since. She is based in Austin and is now scoping the full Q1 release of Insights with engineering.
