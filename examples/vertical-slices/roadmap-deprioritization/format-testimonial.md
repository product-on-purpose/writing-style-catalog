*Featured on meridian.io/customers, added in October.*

"We'd built our board-reporting habit around the Q3 promise: saved views, a scheduled report, no more waiting a week on a support ticket for the numbers. Jordan from customer success called in mid-September to say the dashboard was moving to Q1 2027, and that was not the news we wanted. I would still rather have the finished dashboard, but they told us straight why it slipped and handed us something real to use in the meantime. That is worth recommending on its own."
