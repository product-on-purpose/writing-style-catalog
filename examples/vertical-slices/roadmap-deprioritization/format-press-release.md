**Meridian Ships Insights Data Export, Sets Q1 2027 Dashboard Date**

*Full in-app analytics dashboard now targeted for March 13, 2027*

SAN FRANCISCO, September 26, 2026 - Meridian today released a CSV export of Insights usage data, giving customers direct access to their own account activity ahead of a full in-app analytics dashboard now targeted for March 13, 2027. The export is available immediately through account settings at no added cost. The dashboard was originally committed for Q3 2026; Meridian rescheduled it after a mandatory billing-system migration consumed the engineering capacity allocated to its build.

The export contains one row per usage event, covering everything recorded from account creation through the previous calendar day, with fields for the user and event recorded, a UTC timestamp, the session the event belongs to, and the plan tier active at the time. Customers can open the file in a spreadsheet or business intelligence tool to filter by date range, group activity by user or feature, and build the engagement views they need.

The billing-system migration, required to support Meridian's new plan structure, expanded beyond its original scope this quarter. Shipping the Insights dashboard on the original date would have meant releasing it without saved views or scheduled report delivery, the two capabilities most requested by customers who were given a Q3 commitment. Meridian chose to complete the migration and ship the export now rather than release an incomplete dashboard.

"We would rather put customers' own data in their hands today than ship a dashboard that is missing the two features enterprise teams asked for by name," said Maya Chen, Product Lead, Meridian. "The export draws on the same data layer the full dashboard will use. Customers are not waiting on us to start working with their numbers. They are waiting on the guided views and scheduled reports we are building on top of it."

The Insights dashboard is scheduled for release by March 13, 2027, with date-range selectors, per-feature and per-user breakdowns, saved views, and scheduled summary emails. Engineering design work begins in October, after the billing migration stabilizes in production. Customers with questions about the export or the Q1 2027 timeline can reach Meridian at insights@meridian.io.

About Meridian
Meridian is a software platform that helps product and operations teams manage accounts, billing, and usage analytics in one place. Insights, Meridian's usage-analytics product, gives customers visibility into how their own users engage with their product, from raw event data to the guided reporting built on top of it.
