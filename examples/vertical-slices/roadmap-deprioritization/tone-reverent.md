We made a commitment to you. That commitment - to deliver Insights, a complete in-app analytics dashboard, before the close of Q3 - was made in earnest, and it mattered to us in the making of it. What we owe you now is not a softening of that word, but an honest account of what has happened and what we are going to do about it.

During Q2, a mandatory migration of our core billing infrastructure surfaced requirements that were more extensive than our initial engineering assessment had recognized. We could not defer it; the structural dependencies ran too deep, and proceeding without it would have put customer data and billing integrity at risk. We made the choice to run the migration through, and we completed it. But it consumed the engineering capacity allocated to Insights. What remained was not enough to build it to the standard the commitment had called for.

We will not ship Insights half-built. The thing we promised you - a dashboard through which you can interrogate your data inside the product, without exporting, without workarounds - deserves to be delivered whole or not at all. That standard is not optional. Insights moves to Q1.

We know this lands after you have already planned around our word. Before September closes, we will ship a CSV export of the underlying Insights data, so that what you need to analyze is in your hands while you wait. It is not Insights. We name it here not as a resolution but as a bridge, and we hold the distinction seriously.

The trust you placed in us is not a small thing. We intend to be worthy of it.
