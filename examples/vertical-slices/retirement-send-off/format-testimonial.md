*From the mentee archive Priya Sandhu contributed ahead of Howard's June 25 send-off - Crestfield Group intranet*

"Two years ago a vendor alert fired at 11 p.m. that didn't match anything in our documentation. The on-call runbook stopped at 'escalate to Operations,' which meant Howard, and I didn't want to wake him over something I might be misreading. I called anyway. He asked which utility contact had signed the original contract, a name that existed nowhere in our systems, only in a notebook at his desk. Fixed in twenty minutes. That sequence is written down now, because two other people sat with him for two long sessions and made him say it out loud. I got it a year early, for free, because he picked up the phone. I don't know how you fit twenty-six years into an archive. If something doesn't match the runbook this week, call him anyway. You've still got a few days to be as lucky as I was."
- Priya Sandhu, Operations Analyst, Crestfield Group
