Hi Dana - thanks for posting the whole experience, not just the weeks that held.

Glad the three-line version is working: close the laptop, phone in another room, do not open either until morning. No app, no tracker, nothing to check on the one day you are supposed to stop checking things. That is the design, not a shortcut.

Sorry to hear the streak broke when the deadline landed. Mine did too - the first version ran six weeks before a deadline pulled it under, and I did not restart for eleven months. A broken week is not proof the idea is wrong for you - it is information about which weeks are hardest to protect. The relapse notes (docs/relapse.md) cover what I did with mine.
