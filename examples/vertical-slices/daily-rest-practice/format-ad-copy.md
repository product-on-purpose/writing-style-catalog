# Ad Copy - Growing the One in Seven List

*Three headline tests for a short ad pointing back to the sign-up page, drafted the week the phone-away window went from four hours to ten.*

---

## Variant A - problem hook

**Still checking your phone on your day off?**

One in Seven: fourteen weeks of actually stopping - phone in a drawer, every single week.

**Get Monday's note**

---

## Variant B - outcome hook

**The day that does nothing makes everything else work.**

Fourteen weeks of one real day off a week - read what changed on the other six.

**Read the note**

---

## Variant C - curiosity hook

**I let a work message sit unread for a full day.**

One in Seven is a Monday note about keeping one day a week completely offline.

**Join the list**

---

*Testing all three in the same round. Whichever pulls more sign-ups decides what next Monday's note leads with.*
