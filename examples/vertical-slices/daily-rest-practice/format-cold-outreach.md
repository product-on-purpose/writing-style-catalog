Subject: Your post on the fifteen-minute re-entry

Elena - your post on the first fifteen minutes after a full day offline, the way checking everything at once erases whatever the day just built, is the closest description I have found of what happens to me most Sunday evenings.

I am fourteen weeks into a weekly one-day rest practice - phone away, no messages, no output - and the moment the window closes I scan everything at once and lose whatever the day held. This week I am testing a thirty-minute cap on the re-entry: triage only, no replies.

Did the cap hold for you, or did the scanning find its way back in anyway?

Daniel Weiss
