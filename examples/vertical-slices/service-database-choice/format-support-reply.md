# Support Reply - Delayed Notification Follow-up (June 3 Incident)

Hi Renee,

Thanks for flagging that your comment-mention notification from June 3 arrived almost two hours late - that's not the experience we want, especially when it cost you a deadline.

Here's what happened: a burst of workspace onboarding activity that afternoon pushed our notification queue past its configured capacity for about two hours. Your mention was one of the messages sitting in that backlog, and we delivered it as soon as the queue cleared. Nothing in your workspace was lost or sent twice - the delay was the entire effect, and the rest of your notification history is intact.

We're also closing the gap that let this happen: we're raising the queue's capacity limit and adding write-rate monitoring so a burst like that gets caught before it causes delays, both targeted for June 10.

Let me know if anything else in your notification history looks off, or if you have other questions.
