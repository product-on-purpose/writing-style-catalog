Subject: Your window-light swap for hotel rooms

You wrote about swapping the window-light step for two minutes with every bathroom light on, because your conference hotel had no window that opened. That is the first travel fix I have read that adapts the sequence instead of skipping it for the week.

I have run a four-step version of the same structure (water, light, movement, paper planning) since mid-May. Three travel days this month; I tried to adapt on two of them, both poorly. Your post is the first place I have seen "do not skip, substitute" written down as a rule instead of a slogan.

One question, if you have a spare reply: did you keep all four steps in order, or reorder based on what the room had?
