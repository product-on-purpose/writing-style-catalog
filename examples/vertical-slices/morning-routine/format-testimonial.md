*Left as a reply in the morning-experiment repo's GitHub Discussions, after the Month 1 results post.*

"Before this, my mornings started with the phone already in my hand, and by the time I reached my desk I had doom-scrolled through two news apps and a group chat. A coworker sent me a link to this repo and I tried the four-step version myself: water, light, a short walk, then ten minutes with a notebook before the phone comes out of the kitchen. I am not a routine person - I have said that about myself for years, half as an excuse. Three weeks in, I am still doing it. I have hit all four steps sixteen of the last twenty-one days, and even on the mornings I skip the walk, water and light alone change how the first hour feels. If your day already feels behind before it starts, steal this. It is not complicated. That is the whole point."
- Devon Marsh, IT project coordinator, found the repo through a coworker's Slack link
