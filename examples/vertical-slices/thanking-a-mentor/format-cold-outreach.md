Subject: The line about staying close without taking over

Dana, I read the post you put up last week about the difference between mentoring someone and sponsoring them: staying close enough to catch a real mistake, far enough back that the win stays theirs. I do not know if you remember doing it for me, but that is exactly what you did when you put me on a project I did not feel ready to lead. That was about ten years ago now.

I run a small team myself these days, and this spring I did the same thing for someone on it, before I fully understood what the first version of this had cost you.

Would it be all right if I sent you a longer note about it? A yes or no is plenty.
