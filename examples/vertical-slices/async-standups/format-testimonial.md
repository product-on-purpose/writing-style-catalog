"For a year and a half, our standup landed at 9:30pm for me, right when I wanted to be off the clock and home with my family. I made it to about three out of five weeks most months, and it was never about caring less - it was the clock. When Maya proposed a 30-day async trial, I said yes right away. Now I post my three lines to #team-standup before 10am my time, and if I flag a blocker with an @mention, I usually hear back inside twenty minutes instead of waiting until the next afternoon. I've shown up every single weekday since the trial started, which had never happened for me before. If your team spans time zones, stop asking half of them to stay up late for a status meeting. This is the fix."

- Aditi Sharma, Platform Engineer, Fernbridge Software
