**Employer review site - 4/5 - Current Employee, Software Engineer, India**

**Pros:** Standups stopped happening at 9:30pm my time. Leadership pulled the attendance numbers, saw why the engineers on India time were checked out, and changed the meeting instead of asking us to just show up more.

**Cons:** The async format is still finding its feet. Post something blocking on a day the on-call person is slammed and it can sit for hours before anyone responds.

**Response from Platform Engineering**

Thank you for writing up both the part that's working and the part that hasn't caught up yet. You're right about the old meeting time: it ran on Pacific time by default, and the engineers on India time absorbed that cost every night. Looking at the attendance numbers is what made the problem hard to argue with, and changing the meeting instead of asking people to be more available at 9:30pm was the whole point.

The response-time gap on blocked posts is real and already on our list. We're testing a change to how the on-call rotation triages those posts. A couple of hours of silence on something urgent isn't good enough, new format or not. If you want specifics or want to weigh in, the trial retro doc is open to the team, or bring it to your manager directly.
