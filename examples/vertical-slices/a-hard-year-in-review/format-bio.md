# Marcus Delgado

*marcusdelgado.com - About*

Marcus Delgado is an independent consultant based in Columbus, Ohio, advising public-sector and nonprofit clients on operations, program delivery, and technology adoption. He has run his own practice since 2019, following six years as a program manager at Aldergate Infrastructure Partners, where he coordinated regional infrastructure planning across engineering, policy, and community-relations teams. He holds a B.A. in Public Policy from Ashgrove State University.

From September 2023 to March 2025, Marcus led the Meridian Community Broadband Initiative as a volunteer, directing an eleven-person coalition through an eighteen-month effort to plan and propose community broadband infrastructure. The coalition produced a technically sound proposal and held funder confidence for the full engagement; the initiative closed in March 2025 when its primary funder withdrew, before the work reached deployment. Marcus is currently writing a full account of that closure for the coalition members who built the proposal with him, and is not taking on new large initiatives until it is delivered.
