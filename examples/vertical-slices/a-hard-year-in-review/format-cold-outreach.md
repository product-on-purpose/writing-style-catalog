Subject: The signal I did not escalate in February

Your piece on treating funder disengagement as a detectable pattern instead of a surprise named something I only learned by missing it.

Meridian, the community broadband coalition I led, lost its primary funder in March 2025, four weeks after the first signal appeared and went unescalated. Eleven people had given eighteen months to it. I have since traced exactly where the escalation should have happened and did not.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call on how you decided a signal was real enough to escalate? Not the whole framework, just that one judgment call.

Marcus Delgado, formerly of the Meridian coalition
